### **1. Getting Started**

#### **Logging In**

1. Click the **Login** link in the navigation menu.
2. Enter your **Username** and **Password**.
3. Once logged in, your dashboard view will change based on your assigned permissions (e.g., Student, Staff, or Admin).

#### **Browsing Available Spaces**

* **Home Page:** The homepage displays all "Public" spaces. You can see the name, description, and an image of the area.
* **Viewing Resources:** Click on a space to see the specific resources available within it (e.g., "Room A" might contain "Projector," "Main Desk," etc.).

---

### **2. For Regular Users (Students/Staff)**

#### **Making a Booking**

1. Select the **Space** and then the specific **Resource** you wish to reserve.
2. Click **Book Resource**.
3. Fill out the booking form:
* **Start Time & End Time:** Select your desired timeframe.
* **Attendees:** Enter the number of people expected.


4. **Conflict Check:** The system automatically checks if your time slot overlaps with an already approved booking. If a conflict exists, you will be prompted to choose a different time.
5. Click **Submit**. Your request will be sent to the administrator with a status of **"Pending."**

#### **Managing Your Bookings**

Go to the **My Bookings** section to:

* **Check Status:** See if your request is *Pending*, *Approved*, or *Rejected*.
* **Cancel Booking:** If you no longer need the space, you can cancel a pending or approved request.
* **End Early:** If you finish using a resource before your time expires, click **End Early** to release the resource for others.

---

### **3. For Administrators**

#### **Admin Dashboard**

Access the **Admin Dashboard** to oversee all system activity. Here you can:

* **Review Bookings:** Approve or Reject pending requests.
* **Update Status:** Change a booking to "Draft" if more information is needed.

#### **Asset Management**

Go to **Manage Assets** to configure the system:

* **Spaces:** Create new spaces, edit existing descriptions/images, or delete unused ones.
* **Resources:** Add specific items to spaces (e.g., adding "Workstation 1" to "Lab B").
* **Visibility Toggle:** Use the "Hide/Show" feature to temporarily make a space or resource unavailable to users without deleting it.

#### **User & Permission Management**

* **Add Users:** Register new users and set their initial access level.

### **4. Understanding Booking Statuses**

| Status | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| **Pending** | Your request is submitted and waiting for an admin to review it. |
| **Approved** | Your reservation is confirmed. The resource is locked for your time slot. |
| **Rejected** | The admin has declined the request (usually due to priority or maintenance). |
| **Cancelled** | You have voluntarily retracted the request. |
| **Draft** | The request is being held for further review or modification. |

---

### **5. Troubleshooting & FAQs**

* **"Access Denied" Error:** This means your account does not have the required permission (e.g., trying to book when you only have "View" access). Contact your administrator to update your permission mask.
* **Cannot See a Space:** The administrator may have set the space to "Hidden" for maintenance.
* **Invalid Time Slot:** Ensure your "End Time" is later than your "Start Time." The system will not accept reverse or equal times.
* **Forgotten Password:** If you cannot log in, please reach out to the system administrator to have your password reset.
